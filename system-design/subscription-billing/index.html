Designing a Subscription and Billing System

Patterns for subscription management, metering, invoicing, billing cycles and integrating with payment gateways.

Requirements

  • Support multiple plans, trial periods, upgrades/downgrades
  • Accurate metering for usage-based billing
  • Reliable invoicing and retry (dunning) flows
  • PCI-compliant payment gateway integrations

Architecture

  • Billing service for plan & customer lifecycle
  • Usage collector for events and metering (message queue → aggregator)
  • Scheduler for billing cycles and invoicing
  • Webhooks for payment gateway notifications and reconciliation

Notes

Link to payment gateway design and ensure idempotent operations for external callbacks.
