Fault-Tolerant System Design Patterns

Patterns like circuit breakers, retries with exponential backoff, bulkheads, and graceful degradation for building resilient services.

Patterns

  • Circuit Breaker: stop calling unhealthy services and fallback gracefully
  • Retries with exponential backoff and jitter to avoid thundering herds
  • Bulkheads to isolate failures to subsets of resources
  • Graceful degradation and feature toggles for emergency modes
