Designing a Referral and Affiliate Tracking System

How to track referrals and affiliates: unique tracking links, attribution windows, deduplication, payout calculations and CRM integration.

Requirements

  • Track referral attribution reliably across sessions and channels
  • Prevent fraud and duplicate attributions
  • Support reports for payouts and partner dashboards
  • Integrate with CRM and billing for payouts

Architecture

  • Tracking pixel / link redirects for capturing source and utm-like metadata
  • Event ingestion pipeline (message queue) to process conversions and attribute them
  • Attribution store with TTLs and idempotent writes
  • Fraud detection via scoring and heuristics
