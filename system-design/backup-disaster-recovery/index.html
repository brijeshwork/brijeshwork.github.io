Designing Backup and Disaster Recovery Systems

A practical guide to backups, DR planning, RTO/RPO, failover strategies and runbooks for production systems.

RPO and RTO

Define acceptable data loss (RPO) and restoration time (RTO), and design backup frequency and failover automation to meet those targets.

Strategies

  • Cross-region replication
  • Point-in-time recovery (database snapshots)
  • Cold vs warm vs hot standby
  • Disaster recovery runbooks and regular drills

Conclusion

Recovery is as much people and processes as it is technology; test your runbooks and automate failover where safe and possible.
