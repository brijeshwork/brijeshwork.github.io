Order & Payment Flow Design for E-commerce Platforms

Designing reliable order and payment flows: reservations, two-phase checkout, payment gateway integration, and reconciliation with inventory and billing.

Order flow overview

  1. Customer places order → reserve inventory
  2. Initiate payment with gateway (idempotent request)
  3. On payment success → confirm order and decrement inventory
  4. On payment failure → release reservation and notify customer

Payment integration notes

  • Use webhooks for asynchronous payment confirmations
  • Idempotency keys and retries for safe retries
  • Reconciliation jobs to match payments with orders
