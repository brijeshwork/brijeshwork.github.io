How to Start

Pick a niche

If you want to grow, you can't post many things on one page...

It confuses your followers & algorithm as it won't allow you to rank higher

Also, it is hard to monetize your account all people want clarity...

Follow these steps:

  • Find what you like more..

  • Identify customers' problems and needs. (what people need)

  • Research your competition.

  • Define your account's purpose

  • Test your product or service.

E.g. If you offer Yoga lessons your niche will be Yoga

Describe your niche in one sentence now!

Optimize your profile

10 X 2 Growth strategy

When you accomplish your profile, you need to follow creators related to your niche.

Try this formula: 10 X 2 Growth Strategy

  • Pick 10 accounts in your niche that already have the audience you want.

  • Go to their profiles and turn on notifications and track their behavior.

  • $0,2 cents!! A comment ,a like ,a share, a save, a story share, a comment reply, etc.

  • Repeat this 3-4 times per dayThe secret is to provide value and support, but the game is long-term.

  • Also, apply the same for hashtags..

  • You'll have made over 3,000 connections by the end of the month.

The Best time to post

  • Go to Insights > followers & scroll to the end.

  • Check when is your audience most active

  • & post 1 hour before the peak hour
